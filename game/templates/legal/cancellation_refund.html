Cancellation & Refund Policy

1. Entry Fee Refunds

All entry fees paid for tournaments are non-refundable, except in cases where the tournament is canceled by Bettle Champion due to technical or operational issues. In such cases, full refunds will be provided within 7 business days.

2. Tournament Cancellations

If a tournament is canceled due to technical failures or unforeseen circumstances, we will issue a refund to all participants. You will receive a notification about the cancellation and details about the refund process.

3. Player-Initiated Cancellations

Players may cancel their participation in a tournament before the 48 hours of registration deadline, and in some cases, a refund may be issued depending on the tournament terms. Once the deadline has passed, no refunds will be provided.

4. Processing Refunds

Refunds will be processed through the same payment method used for the original transaction. Please allow up to 7 business days for the refund to reflect in your account.

5. Disputes

In the case of any disputes regarding payments or refunds, please contact our support team at [support email] within 24 hours of the issue.
